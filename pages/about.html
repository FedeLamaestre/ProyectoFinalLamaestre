My name is María Belén Ricca, but you can call me Belu. I'm a content creator, art director and UI UX designer. I am working in the communication industry for more than 5 years.

My journey began in 2016, were I was offered a job for one of the most important global agencies, BBDO. I worked there for 3 years as a Creative Copywriter and Digital Content Creator. Many of my projects have been for highly regarded clients, both local and global brands, such as PepsiCo, Nike, Danone, SC Johnson, Pedigree, among others.

After 3 years working hard, I decided to take a break and move to Australia for 12 months. Experience that helped me to improve the english that I had been learning since school.

Once again in the industry, I worked for a studio in Barcelona. Being part of a small agencie helped me to learn a bit of ever area, which I appreciate a lot.

Now I am working at Palo, a creative agency, as a Content Creator.

I enjoy the communicative world, from the writing to the designing. I feel comfortable, developing concepts, planning strategies, designing posts, creating videos, and executing written and visual content.

If you have something to communicate I'm in.
